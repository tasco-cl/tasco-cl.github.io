About Us

TAQAAT AL SAHRAA CONTRACTING Co.
"Tasco" is a distinguished and renowned Construction and Contracting company with an impeccable service record, outstanding Human Capital Management, and latest technology resources. Established in the year 2009 as contracting establishment for the certain and limited services with respected commercial registration. Later on as the business expended to contribute our professional roll in Saudi Arabia & GCC countries Construction & Contracting Sectors, the TASCO management decided to convert from establishment to a Company having multiple type of Construction and Contracting expertise in different fields under one main Organization's commercial registration. This attainment occurred in May, 2021. and TASCO contracting company fully capable to serve all our respective clients to provide the best possible compatible, quality committed and sustainable Construction&contracting solutions. The company aims to provide quality services for Construction & Contracting, Industrial Support Solutions, Project Management and leasing of all kinds of heavy, light vehicles and equipment for short and long-term basis. TASCO Contracting Co. is involved in turnkey projects consisting of Civil Infrastructure Construction, Electrical, Energy and commissioning works. It has created a position for itself in the Projects supply and applies construction management services. We have completed all our projects with commitment and quality services in various fields of Civil, Electrical, Energy and Mechanical construction & contracting throughout Kingdom of Saudi Arabia with well-known reputed companies.
